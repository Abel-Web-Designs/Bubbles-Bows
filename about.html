About the Owner

Hadley Teague

Hi my name is Hadley Teague. I am 21 years old! I have been grooming dogs for almost 3 years and have had my own shop for almost 2 years! I am the daughter of Chad & Brooke Teague and am the oldest of 3! My sister Campbell (she’s 19) works in the shop with me almost everyday! We have 4 dogs: Ari (pugle basset hound mix), Flint (mini golden doodle), Ollie (English bulldog beagle mix), and Mae (a chocolate lab)! Thank you all for making my little dream of grooming dogs a reality!! I appreciate you all!

We also just recently won Lawrence County’s Best Grooming Salon of 2024!
